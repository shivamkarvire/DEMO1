Why Our Assembly & Soldering Services?

We use a combination of advanced equipment and skilled hand assembly to ensure precision and reliability for every project. Our services include:

Surface Mount Technology (SMT):

Placing and soldering modern, tiny components onto the surface of PCBs with high precision.

Through-Hole Technology (THT):

Ensuring strong mechanical and electrical connections for components with leads.

Prototyping:

We offer fast-turnaround prototyping services to help you test and validate your designs efficiently, moving from concept to reality in a short timeframe.

Quality Assurance:

All completed boards undergo thorough visual and automated inspections to ensure flawless soldering and component placement, meeting or exceeding industry standards.
