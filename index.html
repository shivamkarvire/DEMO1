SINGLE LAYER PCBs

A single-layer PCB is a circuit board with a single conductive copper layer on one side of a non-conductive substrate, making it simple and inexpensive to manufacture.

DOUBLE LAYER PCBs

A double-layer PCB (also known as a two-layer or double-sided PCB) is a printed circuit board with a conductive copper layer on both its top and bottom surfaces.
