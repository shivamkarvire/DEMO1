SINGLE LAYER PCBs

A Single Layer PCB (Printed Circuit Board), also known as a Single-Sided PCB, is the simplest type of PCB where all the conductive components and circuitry are mounted on one side of a non-conductive substrate.

DOUBLE LAYER PCBs

Double Layer PCBs have conductive copper layers on both sides, offering more complex routing and functionality compared to single-sided PCBs.
